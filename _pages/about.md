I am currently pursuing my **MSc in Artificial Intelligence** at **The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)**. Previously, I received my **B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence** from **Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)** in 2025.

My research interests center on **large language models (LLMs)** and **agentic AI** — in particular, reinforcement learning during post-training (RLHF, RLAIF, RLVR, DPO and beyond), multimodal foundation models, and LLM agents that reason, plan, and act.

I am actively looking for collaborations and research opportunities in these directions. Feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:feiyanghuang@link.cuhk.edu.hk).
