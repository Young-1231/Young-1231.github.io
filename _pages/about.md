I am currently a graduate student in the **MSc in Artificial Intelligence** program at **The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)**. Prior to joining CUHK, I received my **B.Eng. degree in Artificial Intelligence** from **Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)**. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to work with Professor Hao Lu at HUST.

My research interests lie in **multimodal large language models (MLLMs)**, **post-training of foundation models**, and the **interpretability of vision-language models during post-training**. I am open to collaborative research opportunities.

- **B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence**, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, 2021 – 2025
